02. about me

i operate at the unique intersection of creative development and engineering execution. my expertise spans the complete pipeline, from crafting high-fidelity 3d animations and digital twin architectures to designing and fabricating physical prototypes.

my professional experience at via design architects focused on r&d, including developing custom python toolsets for revit (pyrevit), leading the firm's first digital twin initiative, and integrating live iot sensor data for real-time asset tracking.
